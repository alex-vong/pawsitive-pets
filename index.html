Pawsitive Impact  Adoption Center

Pawsitive Pets is a locally run animal adoption and humane center that focuses on the well-being, health, and love of stray animals.

We believe every pet deserves and owner but not every owner deserves a pet. At Pawsitive we go through vigorous screening to ensure all our animals go to the pawsitively best owners pawsible.
